Podcasts

If you regularly produce a podcast, we can help you create fresh & engaging content divorced listeners will love.

If you've never worked one-on-one with divorced women before, you might be surprised how many of them turn to podcasts for support during and after their divorce.

Divorce can be a lonely and bewildering experience, and, in contrast to more traditional "print media", podcasts offer women a chance to literally hear from others who've faced similar challenges and grappled with similar fears.

Compatible Connections is open to working with podcasters to develop content that will drive engagement and grow their pool of regular listeners.

You can find examples of possible podcast topics, as well as recordings of previous podcast collaborations, below.
